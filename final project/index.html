White sauce pasta

#Ingredients

  • 2 cups pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

#Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the flour and cook for about a minute.
  5. Gradually add the milk, whisking continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook until the sauce thickens, then add cheese, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  7. Add the cooked pasta and vegetables (if using) to the sauce and mix well.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with extra cheese or herbs if desired.
